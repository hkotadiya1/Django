About Us

Welcome to NKB Exim

NKB Exim is an Indian registered Company based in Rajkot. We are dealing with HMS Scrap (Steel Scrap), Cast Iron Scrap, Aluminium Scrap, Copper Scrap and Plastic Scrap. The company is in international trading since 2022. NKB Exim was started by Mr. Harshal Kotadiya, Mr. Jagram Rewar and Mr. Vipul Kotadiya, with a vision to be one of the topmost Steel and commodities trading companies in India.

We supply material for the daily requirements for steel, steel rolling mills, cast iron or melting foundries, and material recycling companies across the world. Our team has industry professionals with vast experience to procure material from various sources across the world to meet our diversified customers requirements.

Mission

It is our mission to treat our customers & suppliers with honesty and respect. Our commitment to providing the industry with high quality and sustainable scrap material.

Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in the scrap industry and to upload our quality at every stage and maintain consistency with new innovative ideas to win the trust of our valued customers worldwide.

Goal

Our goal is to Increase efficiency, Provide better customer service, Increase profit margin, and Capture a bigger market share.
